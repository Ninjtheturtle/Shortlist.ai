How Our AI Works

Three pillars of our intelligent hiring platform

Vector Similarity Matching

Candidate profiles and job requirements are embedded into 768-dimensional vectors using our fine-tuned language model.

  • Cosine similarity: (A·B)/(||A|| ||B||)
  • Top 5% candidates automatically advanced

AI-Conducted Interviews

Natural conversations powered by our voice engine with dynamic question generation.

  • Behavioral: "Tell me about a difficult decision you made"
  • Technical: Domain-specific problem solving

Conversation Analytics

Every interview is processed through our multimodal analysis pipeline.

  • Vocal analysis: confidence, clarity, tone
  • Content analysis: knowledge, relevance
  • Composite score (0-100) with ranking
